About Us

Our Mission

Leya Ship Management is dedicated to connecting qualified seafarers with reputable vessel owners worldwide. We strive to provide exceptional crew management services with integrity, professionalism, and attention to detail.

Our Vision

To become the leading crew management agency known for excellence in maritime staffing solutions, creating valuable opportunities for seafarers while meeting the highest standards of vessel owners.

Established

2023

Our Approach

At Leya Ship Management, we understand that the success of any vessel depends on its crew. That's why we take a meticulous approach to crew selection, ensuring that each seafarer meets not only the technical qualifications but also possesses the right attitude and work ethic.

We maintain close relationships with both seafarers and vessel owners, creating a reliable bridge between talent and opportunity in the maritime industry.
